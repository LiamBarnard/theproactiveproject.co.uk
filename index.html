About Me

Hi, my name is John Mercer, first and foremost I am a father to my incredible son Troy and husband to my amazing wife Laura. My “9-5” is that I work for the East of England ambulance service as an Emergency Medical Technician, I really do enjoy helping people and, in this role, it allows me to do so on an emergency reactive basis. I have always had a passion for health and food, I noticed that there was a severe lack of good, honest information that was readily available to help people with their nutrition.

That’s why I created The Proactive Project, with the intention that anyone who was looking to positively impact their health through nutrition should be able too. That’s why my advice on nutrition, meal plans and general tips and guidance will ALWAYS be free to anyone that requests it.

In my journey of my own health and wellbeing goals I have found some products that I myself use to this day, depending on your goals I may advocate the use of some of these products however this is completely down to you as an individual. If you have any queries please feel free to reach out using any of my social media accounts.
